TechNova Shipping and Warranty Information

Shipping Options and Costs
- Standard shipping: 5 to 7 business days, FREE on all orders.
- Express shipping: 2 to 3 business days, $15 flat.
- Next-day shipping: order before 12:00 PM EST, $30 flat (available in the US only).

Order Processing
Orders are processed within 1 business day. You will receive a tracking number
by email once the order ships. Orders placed on weekends are processed the next
business day.

International Shipping
TechNova currently ships to the US, Canada, the UK, and the UAE. International
orders may take 10 to 14 business days and can incur customs duties paid by the
customer. Free standard shipping does not apply to international orders; a flat
$25 fee is charged.

Warranty Coverage
Every TechNova product includes a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing
defects. This does not cover accidental damage, water damage beyond the rated
resistance, or unauthorized repairs.

NovaCare+ Extended Warranty
For extra protection, you can purchase NovaCare+ within 60 days of buying a
product:
- NovaCare+ for laptops: $199 for 2 additional years, includes 2 accidental
  damage claims per year with a $99 service fee each.
- NovaCare+ for phones: $99 for 2 additional years, includes 2 accidental
  damage claims per year with a $49 service fee each.

Warranty Claims
To file a claim, open the NovaCare app or contact support with your order number
and a description of the issue. Approved claims are repaired or replaced within
7 to 10 business days.
