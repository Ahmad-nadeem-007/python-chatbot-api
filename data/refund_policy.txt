TechNova Refund and Return Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

30-Day Return Window
Customers may return any TechNova product within 30 days of the delivery date
for a full refund, provided the product is in its original condition with all
accessories and packaging. The delivery date is the date shown as "Delivered"
in your order tracking.

How to Start a Return
1. Log in to your TechNova account and open "My Orders".
2. Select the order and click "Request Return".
3. Choose a reason and print the prepaid return shipping label.
4. Drop the package at any authorized courier location.

Refund Processing Time
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within
5 to 7 business days. The amount is credited back to the original payment
method. Bank processing may add another 3 to 5 business days.

Restocking Fee
There is NO restocking fee for returns made within the 30-day window. Returns
requested after 30 days are handled case by case and may incur a 15% fee.

Non-Returnable Items
- Gift cards and digital NovaCloud subscriptions are non-refundable.
- Software licenses that have been activated cannot be returned.
- Products with physical damage caused by the customer are not eligible.

Damaged or Defective on Arrival
If a product arrives damaged or defective, contact support within 7 days for a
free replacement or full refund, including any shipping costs. This is separate
from the standard 30-day return window.

Exchanges
We do not offer direct exchanges. To switch to a different product, return the
original item for a refund and place a new order.
