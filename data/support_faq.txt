TechNova Customer Support FAQ

Support Hours
Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM (EST).
We are closed on Sundays and major public holidays. Live chat and email are
monitored during these hours; phone support is Monday to Friday only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my NovaBook if it freezes?
A: Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds until the screen turns off,
   then press it again to restart. No data is lost during a force restart.

Q: I forgot my NovaCloud password. What should I do?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". A reset link is sent to
   your registered email and is valid for 30 minutes.

Q: Can I use a NovaPhone charger to charge my NovaBook?
A: No. The NovaBook requires a 65W USB-C adapter. The NovaPhone charger is 30W
   and will charge very slowly or not at all.

Q: How do I check my warranty status?
A: Open the NovaCare app, go to "My Devices", and your warranty end date is
   shown under each product.

Q: Does TechNova offer data transfer from my old device?
A: Yes. The free NovaMigrate tool transfers photos, contacts, and files. Visit
   any TechNova store or use the in-app guide.

Q: Is there a student discount?
A: Yes, students get 10% off with a valid student ID, verified at checkout.
